Selected Assets in the City of Greater Bendigo, Australia

LAYER INFORMATION

The datasets on this website are collections of geospatial data that has been compiled by the Australian Government Public Data for use in mapping applications and data analysis.

The data show some selected assets, such as Preschools and Childcare Centres, Recreation Playspaces, Public Roads and Garbage Collection Zones in the City of Greater Bendigo, Australia.
It is meant to be used to help users visualize and understand some of the assets in the city.

The main characteristics and basic information of the different assets are presented below:

Preschools and Childhood Centers

This dataset indicates Preschools and Childcare Centres within the City of Greater Bendigo. The data is from the open data of the Local Government.
It was published on 21st November, 2018 and updated on 26th December, 2022.
Preschools and Childhood Centers are light red point markers on the map.

Recreation Playspaces

This dataset indicates Public Playspaces within the City of Greater Bendigo. The data is from the open data of the Local Government.
It was published on 17th September, 2017 and updated on 26th December, 2022.
Recreation Playspaces are red point markers on the map.

Public Roads

This dataset indicates Public roads in respect of which the City of Greater Bendigo is the Coordinating Road Authority. The data is from the open data of the Local Government.
It was published on 31st August, 2017 and updated on 26th December, 2022.
Public Roads are blue lines on the map.

Garbage Collection Zones

This dataset indicates Waste collection zones within the City of Greater Bendigo, including garbage, recycling, organics and commercial collection. The data is from the open data of the Local Government.
It was published on 21st July, 2016 and updated on 26th December, 2022.
Garbage Collection Zones are multicoloured areas on the map.

Green area is collected on Fridays,
Yellow area is collected on Wednesdays,
Orange area is collected on Thursdays,
Blue area is collected on Tuesdays and
Purple area is collected on Tuesdays
